Tee's Cakery Recipe

Baking ingredients

  • 1 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, large
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups of flour, unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Baking instructions

  1. heat oven to 375dc
  2. stir flour with baking soda and salt;
  3. in large bowl, beat butter with sugar
  4. add egg and vanilla one at time. mix at low speed
  5. gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture then stir
  6. make sure when you cook your cake is fluff and sponge
